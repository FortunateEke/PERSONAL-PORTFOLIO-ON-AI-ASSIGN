About Me

I am Fortunate Uche Eke, a Health Information Management Officer with a passion for leveraging technology to solve real-world problems, especially within the healthcare sector. My professional journey has been centered on managing health information systems, ensuring data accuracy, supporting clinical decision-making, and promoting efficient healthcare delivery. Working in healthcare has given me a firsthand understanding of the critical role that information, innovation, and digital transformation play in improving lives and strengthening institutions. Driven by a desire to bridge the gap between healthcare and technology, I enrolled in the Full-Stack Development Program at Turning Tech Academy, an initiative powered by the Enugu State Government. The program has provided me with the opportunity to build practical skills in modern web technologies, software development, and problem-solving while connecting with a community of forward-thinking innovators. My transition into software development is fueled by a clear vision: to create impactful digital solutions that address challenges in healthcare, education, and society at large. I believe that combining domain expertise in health information management with technical skills in full-stack development positions me to build systems that are not only functional but also meaningful and user-centered. As a developer, I am continuously learning and exploring technologies that enable scalable, accessible, and efficient solutions. From crafting responsive user interfaces to understanding backend architectures, I embrace every opportunity to grow, innovate, and contribute to the evolving digital landscape. I am passionate about lifelong learning, collaboration, and using technology as a tool for positive change. My goal is to help shape a future where healthcare and technology work seamlessly together to improve outcomes, empower communities, and create lasting impact. When I'm not coding or working with health records, you'll find me researching emerging technologies, doing some AI automations, exploring remote opportunities, and building projects that reflect my commitment to innovation and continuous improvement. Let's connect, collaborate, and build solutions that make a difference.
